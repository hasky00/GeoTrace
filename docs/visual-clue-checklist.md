# Visual Clue Checklist

This checklist helps GeoTrace users record visible clues from a photo when EXIF, GPS, or camera metadata is missing.

The goal is not to guess too quickly. The goal is to collect evidence carefully, organize it, and use it to build candidate locations.

## 1. Text, Language, and Signs

Record any visible text.

- Street signs
- Business names
- Road signs
- Warning signs
- Menus or posters
- License plates
- Public transport signs
- Brand names
- Language or alphabet used

## 10. Confidence Rules

Use confidence carefully.

- **Low confidence**: weak clues, many possible locations
- **Medium confidence**: several clues point to one region
- **High confidence**: multiple independent clues support the same location

Do not claim certainty unless the evidence is strong and independently verified.
