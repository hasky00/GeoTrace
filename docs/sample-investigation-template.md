# Sample Investigation Template

Use this template to document a GeoTrace photo-location investigation.

This template is designed for cases where EXIF, GPS, or camera metadata may be missing. It helps keep the investigation structured, transparent, and evidence-based.

## 11. Responsible Use Notes

- Do not claim certainty from weak evidence.
- Separate observations from assumptions.
- Do not publish private or sensitive images without permission.
- Avoid exposing personal information.
- Keep alternative candidates visible.
- Update the investigation when new evidence appears.
